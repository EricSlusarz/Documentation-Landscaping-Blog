# Blogging Business Documentation for J & JJ Landscape

Project documentation uses Markdown.

---

## Overview

This documentation will be a resource for any J&JJ Landscaping employee wishing to perform any function on the Blogging Platform. This site documents the goals and reasoning behind implementing a blogging platform and how to succeful operate the site for achieving the best results for J&JJ Landscaping.

---
## Blog Goals

- More Traffic
- More Leads
- More Sales

## Strategies

1. Have over 311 Indexed Pages in Google 
    - [Study Shows that having over 311+ indexed pages drives Median Monthly Leads by Triple Compared to the 176-310 Indexed Pages Grouping](https://blog.hubspot.com/blog/tabid/6307/bid/5806/Study-Shows-More-Pages-Indexed-by-Google-Means-More-Leads.aspx)
2. Post Regularly (Choose weekly or Bi-weekly)
3. Add Social Share Buttons (Choose which social media sites to share to)
4. Keyword Research on Each Blog Post
5. Schedule Content to post Automatically
6. Have a back log of content (weeks to a year)
