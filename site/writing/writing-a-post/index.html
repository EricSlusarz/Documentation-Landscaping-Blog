What makes a good blog post?

Do Not:

  1. No call to action
  2. No catchy headlines
  3. No images

Do:

  1. Catchy Attractive Headline (question, top 5, etc)
  2. Catchy Picture
  3. Introdction that hooks
  4. Main body that addresses the thesis
  5. Clean Conclusion
  6. Call To Action (Contact Us, Comment Below, Share your Story, Deal or offer)
    1. Contact us
    2. Comment Below
    3. Share your story
    4. Deal or Offer
  7. Sharing options
    1. Social Media
    2. Related Posts
    3. Comments
